# Privacy Policy for ZenChess - Anxiety Free Chess

**Last updated**: February 2026

ZenChess ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension, ZenChess - Anxiety Free Chess, handles data.

## 1. Information We Collect
**Short answer:** None.

ZenChess does not collect, track, transmit, or store any of your personal information, browsing history, chess account details, or gameplay data.

## 2. How We Use Permissions
The extension requests the following permission, which is used strictly for local functionality:
- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save your toggle preferences (e.g., whether you have chosen to hide Homepage Stats or Chat Ratings) so your settings persist across browsing sessions. This preference data is stored locally in your browser's Chrome storage.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Because we collect no data, we do not and cannot share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

## 4. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we have no way to contact our users, any modifications will be reflected directly on this page via our GitHub repository. 

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/art3mes/ZenChess).
